Membership Levels and Benefits

NP Membership

Specifically tailored for non-profit organizations, this membership level is designed to support these entities by providing them with access to valuable resources and networking opportunities at no cost.

  • Access to networking events and community gatherings.
  • Free or discounted access to workshops and seminars aimed at non-profit management and growth.
  • Inclusion in the Chamber's non-profit directory.
  • Opportunities for spotlight features in Chamber communications to highlight your non-profit's work and needs.

Bronze Membership

Ideal for small businesses or startups looking to gain visibility and make the most of networking opportunities.

  • Basic listing in the Chamber's online business directory.
  • Access to members-only networking events.
  • Discounted rates for business training sessions and workshops.
  • Monthly newsletter featuring updates, opportunities, and advertising space.

Silver Membership

Designed for established businesses looking to expand their reach and influence within the community.

  • All the benefits of Bronze Membership.
  • Enhanced listing in the Chamber's online business directory with priority over Bronze listings.
  • Free advertising space in the Chamber's newsletter twice a year.
  • Access to advanced training sessions and exclusive events.
  • One spotlight position on the homepage annually to showcase your business.

Gold Membership

The premium level of membership, offering the highest level of visibility, access, and exclusivity, tailored for leaders and influencers in the business community.

  • All the benefits of Silver Membership.
  • Top-tier listing in the Chamber's online business directory.
  • Free advertising space in the Chamber's newsletter quarterly.
  • Priority access to all Chamber events, including VIP events and roundtables.
  • Quarterly spotlight positions on the homepage.
  • Exclusive discounts for event sponsorships, offering significant visibility at Chamber events.
