Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name: Kumasi Chamber of Commerce

We carefully selected the site name, "Kumasi Chamber of Commerce," to not only signify the city's location but also to illustrate its rich cultural and linguistic nuances.

The name Kumasi was derived from the Twi language, meaning 'the Kum tree survived' or 'under the Kum tree'. The word "asi" means 'under', 'down', 'below' or 'something that has endured or survived'.

Site Purpose

Economic Catalyst

The Kumasi Chamber of Commerce is a regional economic booster by fostering business growth and development in the city. The website will provide a platform for the city services, business promotion, collaboration, and to highlight of Kumasi attractivity.

Community Development

Beyond business, the chamber actively engages in community development and advocates for a balanced economic prosperity. The website will serve as a crossroad for information on community initiatives, events and development.

Scenarios

Business Networking Events

What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

The Events section in the Discover page provides a calendar that highlights upcoming networking events, seminars, and business meet-ups organized by the chamber.

Contact Information for the Board of Directors

Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

The Directory page includes extensive information about the board of directors, including their names, positions and contact details.

Population Growth Data

What has been the population growth in the area?

The Discover page provides access to relevant data, including current demographic, local history and other information influencing the community.

Join the Community

How can I join the community of the Chamber of Commerce?

The Join Us page provides details on three membership: Non-Profit, Silver, and Gold. It includes an application form and highlights the unique benefits associated with each membership level.
